Meet Donna

About Grandma’s Garden

Hello, friend. I am Donna, the grandma behind Grandma’s Garden on Long Island. This little business is my way of sharing health, wellness, and culinary delights with neighbors who feel like family. I host gardening workshops for adults and homeschoolers, I offer garden consults when you want a gentle nudge in the right direction, and I am a proud grandma to two beautiful grandchildren who remind me every day why good food and good soil matter.

I grow everything without harsh chemicals, and I pick what I can right from my own backyard. The honey you see here is raw and locally sourced from Long Island hives. Garlic, lavender, elderberries, hot peppers, and herbs spend their days outside with the birds and the breeze before they ever land in a jar.

When you open one of my syrups, honeys, or small batch blends, I hope it feels like a kitchen hug. If you have questions about a recipe, a plant, or a workshop, reach out. I love this work, and I love getting to grow it alongside you.
